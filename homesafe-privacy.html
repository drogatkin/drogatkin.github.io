Privacy policy for Android app HomeSafe

HomeSafe doesn't collect or use any user's location data beyond required the application functionality.

Such location data isn't store anywhere besides of the application itself.

The same is true for e-mails, or phone numbers.

Say more, HomeSafe doesn't use Internet at all, so location data can't leak anyway.
